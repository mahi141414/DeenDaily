Render + Convex + YouTube Shorts

Queue a video, close the tab, come back later to uploads.

Paste a YouTube URL and the worker will split it into shorts, upload them automatically, and retry blocked uploads one hour later.

Persistent queue in Convex
State survives Render restarts.

What happens

The worker fetches subtitles, finds 10 shorts, cuts them, uploads them, and updates the queue as it goes.

Retry logic

If YouTube blocks uploads with uploadLimitExceeded, the job waits one hour and resumes from the last saved segment.
